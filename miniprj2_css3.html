Splitboard

After watching the netflix documentary Art of Flight I sat back in a gaze and daydreamed of knee deep powder and fresh tree runs, with the snow at my back and in my face. This brought me to my web browser where I began to search the interwebs for a new snowboard for the 2021 season. Now realizing it has been 8 years since I purchased my last snowboard, I have no idea what model to look for, better yet, what website to navigate to.

This brings me to what this ramble is about; split boards. A splitboard is a type of snowboard that can be split in two and used as skiis in order to hike uphill and on flat terrain. The bindings move into an alternative position and with the help of ski poles one is good to start hiking on this unique 2-in-1 floatation device. No more bulky snowshoes needed on future backcountry snowboarding trips. This is definitely the type of board I will be rocking this season.

Floatation devices
Any type of foot gear that straps to the feet that reduces the amount of sinking into snow, specifically powdery snow.
